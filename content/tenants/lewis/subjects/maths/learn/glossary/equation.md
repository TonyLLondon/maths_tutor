### In plain English

An **equation** is a maths sentence that says two things are **equal**. It has an equals sign. Often you find the value of a letter that makes the sentence true.

### Example

$x + 7 = 12$ is an equation. When $x = 5$, both sides are $12$, so $x = 5$ is the solution.

### Watch out

- An **expression** has no equals sign; an **equation** does.
- Whatever you do to one side of an equation, do the **same** to the other to keep it balanced.
