### In plain English

The **mean** is the **average** you get by adding all the values and dividing by how many there are. It is one way to describe a “typical” value in a data set.

### Example

Scores $4, 5, 7$: sum $= 16$, there are $3$ scores, mean $= 16 \div 3 \approx 5.3$.

### Watch out

- One very large or very small value can **pull** the mean up or down.
- Mean is not the same as **median** or **mode**.
