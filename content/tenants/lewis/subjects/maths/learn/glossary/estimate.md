### In plain English

An **estimate** is a **sensible guess** for an answer that you work out quickly in your head or with rounded numbers. It helps you see if a full calculation is reasonable.

### Example

To estimate $49 \times 21$, use $50 \times 20 = 1000$. The exact answer is $1029$, which is close to $1000$, so your working looks believable.

### Watch out

- Estimates do not need to be exact, but they should be **in the right ballpark**.
- Say when you are **rounding** — that makes your estimate clear.
