### In plain English

A **formula** is a rule written with letters that tells you how to **calculate** something. You plug in numbers for the letters to get an answer.

### Example

The area of a rectangle is $A = l \times w$. If length $l = 8$ cm and width $w = 3$ cm, then $A = 24$ cm².

### Watch out

- Check which letters mean what (length, width, radius, and so on).
- A formula is not the same as an **equation** to solve — often you just substitute and calculate.
