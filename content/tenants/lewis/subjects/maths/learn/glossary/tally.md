### In plain English

A **tally** is a quick way to **count** by drawing marks in groups of five. The fifth mark crosses the previous four so you can see totals at a glance.

### Example

You ask $7$ people if they prefer cats or dogs. For cats you draw **||||** then **||** — seven marks — and write the frequency $7$ beside it.

### Watch out

- Bundle in **fives** so you miscount less often.
- The tally count must match the **frequency** in your table.
