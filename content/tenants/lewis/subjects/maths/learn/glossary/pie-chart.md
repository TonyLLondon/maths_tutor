### In plain English

A **pie chart** is a circle split into **slices** to show how a whole is shared between categories. Bigger slices mean a larger fraction of the total.

### Example

In a class of $30$, $15$ walk to school and $15$ come by bus. Half and half means each slice is $180°$ if you draw a pie chart with two equal parts.

### Watch out

- All slices together should make the **full $360°$** (or $100\%$).
- Read whether the question gives **angles**, **percentages**, or **counts** — you may need to convert.
