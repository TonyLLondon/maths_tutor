### In plain English

**Area** is the amount of **flat space** inside a shape. It is measured in square units, like cm² or m². Bigger area means more surface covered.

### Example

A rectangle $4$ cm by $5$ cm has area $4 \times 5 = 20$ cm². That is the number of $1$ cm by $1$ cm squares that would fit inside.

### Watch out

- Area is **not** the same as **perimeter** (distance around the edge).
- Always use **square** units for area, not ordinary length units.
