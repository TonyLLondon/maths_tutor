### In plain English

**Frequency** is how **often** something happens, or how **many times** a value appears in a list or table. In a tally chart, the frequency is the count for each category.

### Example

Test scores are $7, 8, 8, 9, 8, 7$. The frequency of $8$ is $3$ because $8$ appears three times.

### Watch out

- **Frequency** is a count; **frequency density** is used in some grouped charts — read the question carefully.
- The total of all frequencies should match how many results you collected.
