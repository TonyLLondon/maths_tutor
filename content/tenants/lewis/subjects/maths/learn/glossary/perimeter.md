### In plain English

The **perimeter** is the **total distance around** the outside edge of a shape. You add up the lengths of all the sides.

### Example

A rectangle has sides $5$ cm and $3$ cm. Perimeter $= 5 + 3 + 5 + 3 = 16$ cm — the distance you would walk around the edge.

### Watch out

- Perimeter uses **length** units (cm, m); **area** uses square units.
- For a circle, the perimeter is called the **circumference**.
