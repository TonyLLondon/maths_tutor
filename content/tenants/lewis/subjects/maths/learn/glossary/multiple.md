### In plain English

A **multiple** is what you get when you multiply a number by a whole number ($1, 2, 3, \ldots$). Multiples of $5$ are $5, 10, 15, 20$, and so on.

### Example

$24$ is a multiple of $8$ because $8 \times 3 = 24$. It is also a multiple of $4$ and $6$.

### Watch out

- Every number is a **multiple of itself** and a multiple of $1$.
- **Multiple** (bigger steps) is different from **factor** (divides exactly into the number).
