### In plain English

A **percentage** is parts out of **one hundred**. The symbol is **%**. $50\%$ means $50$ out of $100$, which is the same as half.

### Example

$20\%$ of $60$ is $\frac{20}{100} \times 60 = 12$. You can think: one percent of $60$ is $0.6$, times twenty is $12$.

### Watch out

- $100\%$ is the **whole**; $10\%$ is one tenth.
- “Increase by $10\%$” is not the same as “$10\%$ of” — read the wording carefully.
