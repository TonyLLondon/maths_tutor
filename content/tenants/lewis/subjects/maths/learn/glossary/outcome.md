### In plain English

An **outcome** is one **possible result** of a chance experiment. When you roll a die, each face showing is one outcome.

### Example

When you flip a coin, the outcomes are **heads** and **tails**. When you roll a standard die, there are six outcomes: $1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6$.

### Watch out

- List **single** results — “heads” is an outcome; “heads or tails” is not one outcome.
- All outcomes together make the **sample space**.
