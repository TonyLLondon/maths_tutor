### In plain English

A **sequence** is an **ordered list** of numbers (or shapes) that follow a rule. You find the next term from the pattern or from a formula.

### Example

$3, 7, 11, 15, \ldots$ adds $4$ each time. The next term is $19$. The rule could be “start at $3$, add $4$ repeatedly”.

### Watch out

- The **nth term** formula lets you jump to any position without listing every number.
- Some sequences go **up**, some **down**, some **multiply** instead of add.
