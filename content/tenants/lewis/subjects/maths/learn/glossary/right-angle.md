### In plain English

A **right angle** is exactly **$90°$**. It looks like the corner of a square or a book. Two lines that meet at a right angle are **perpendicular**.

### Example

The letter “L” shape at the corner is a right angle. On many diagrams a small square in the corner marks a right angle without writing $90°$.

### Watch out

- A right angle is neither **acute** (smaller) nor **obtuse** (larger).
- Angles in a triangle can include a right angle — then it is a **right-angled triangle**.
