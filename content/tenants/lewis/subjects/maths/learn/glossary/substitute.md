### In plain English

To **substitute** means to **replace a letter** with a number and then calculate. You plug the value in everywhere that letter appears.

### Example

If $y = 2x + 1$ and $x = 4$, substitute: $y = 2 \times 4 + 1 = 9$.

### Watch out

- Use **brackets** when you substitute negative numbers: $x = -3$ in $x^2$ means $(-3)^2 = 9$.
- Substitute into **expressions** and **formulas**; solving an **equation** means finding the unknown letter.
