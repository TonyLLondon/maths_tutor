## What lives here

Probability is about **chance**: how likely something is to happen, or how often it happens when you try again and again. You might talk about whether it will rain, who wins a game, or what colour counter you pick from a bag. In this area you learn to count outcomes fairly, write chances as fractions or decimals, and combine simple events without guessing.

## You might already know

- Words like **impossible**, **possible**, and **certain** for everyday events.
- That a fair coin has two sides and each side is as likely as the other.
- How to write a simple fraction, like “3 out of 10”.
- That rolling a normal dice gives a number from 1 to 6.
- That “more likely” means something happens more often if everything is fair.
