## Reasoning (AO2)

100. Archer says $10^{-2} = -100$. Is he correct? Explain.

101. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $a^0 = 1$ for any non-zero number $a$.

102. Sloan says $(2^3)^2 = 2^5$. Is she correct? Explain.

103. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $\sqrt{a} \times \sqrt{a} = a$ for $a \geq 0$.

104. Explain what a negative index means, using $2^{-3}$ as an example.

105. **Always, sometimes, or never?** A negative index always gives a negative number.

106. Compare $10^2 \times 10^{-4}$ and $10^{-2}$. Are they equal? Explain.

107. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $x^a \div x^b = x^{a-b}$ when $x \neq 0$.

108. Archer says $0^{-1} = 0$. Is he correct? Explain.

109. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $(x^2)^3 = x^5$.

110. Explain what $10^{-1}$ means, as a fraction and as a decimal.

111. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $2^2 \times 3^2 = 6^2$.

112. Sloan says $\sqrt{0.25} = 0.25^{1/2}$. Is she correct? Explain.

113. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Dividing by $10^n$ is the same as multiplying by $10^{-n}$.

114. Explain why $\sqrt[3]{8} = 2$.

115. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $a^{-2} = -a^2$.

116. Compare $4^{1/2}$ and $4^{-1/2}$. What do you notice?

117. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $(ab)^2 = a^2 b^2$.

118. Sloan writes $x^3 \div x^5 = x^2$. Explain the mistake and give the correct answer.

119. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $10^x \times 10^y = 10^{xy}$.

120. Explain what $25^{1/2}$ means and find its value.

121. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $2^{2n} = (2^2)^n$.

122. Sloan says $\dfrac{1}{3^{-2}} = 9$. Is she correct? Explain.

123. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $0^{-1}$ is defined in school maths.

124. Explain the difference between $2^3$ and $2^{1/3}$.

125. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $x^{-1} = \dfrac{1}{x}$ for $x \neq 0$.

126. Archer says $10^0 = 10$. Is he correct? Explain.

127. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $(-2)^{-2}$ is negative.

128. Explain why $\sqrt{100}$ and $100^{1/2}$ mean the same thing.

129. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $m^2 \times m^3 = m^6$.
