## Reasoning (AO2)

100. Priya works out 8 + 12 ÷ 4 and gets 5 by adding first. Explain her mistake and give the correct answer.
101. **Always, sometimes, or never?** In $a + b × c$, working left to right gives the same answer as multiplying first.
102. Sloan says $5² − 4²$ equals $(5 − 4)²$. Is she correct? Show both values.
103. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Brackets around a single number change the value of a calculation.
104. Archer calculates $20 − 5 × 2$ as $(20 − 5) × 2$. Is he correct? Work out both values.
105. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $a ÷ b + c$ gives the same answer as $a ÷ (b + c)$.
106. Kim says that in $3 + 4 × 5$, the 4 and 5 must be multiplied before adding 3 because multiplication is stronger. Is Kim's reasoning correct?
107. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $(a + b)²$ equals $a² + b²$.
108. Explain why $6 ÷ 2 × 3$ is 9, not 1.
109. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Doing powers before multiplication changes every calculation that has both.
110. Mi works out $10 − 3 − 2$ as 5 by subtracting 3 from 10, then 2 from 7. Is Mi correct?
111. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $2 × 3²$ equals $(2 × 3)²$.
112. Sloan thinks $1 + 2 × 3 × 4$ means $(1 + 2) × (3 × 4)$. What is the correct value?
113. **Always, sometimes, or never?** You can remove brackets from $(5 + 0) × 3$ without changing the answer.
114. Archer says $100 ÷ 10 ÷ 2$ means $100 ÷ (10 ÷ 2)$. Is he correct? Work out both values.
115. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Squaring a negative number gives a negative result.
116. Explain why brackets matter in $30 − (2 + 3) × 4$ but not in $30 − 2 + 3$.
117. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $0 × 5 + 3$ equals $0 × (5 + 3)$.
118. Priya inserts brackets in $12 ÷ 4 + 2$ to get 2. Where do the brackets go?
119. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $(10 − 4) ÷ 2$ equals $10 − 4 ÷ 2$.
120. Kim claims $3 + 2³ = 5³$. Explain the mistake and give the correct value.
121. **Always, sometimes, or never?** In $a − b − c$, you must work from left to right.
122. Archer evaluates $(8 − 3)²$ as $8² − 3²$. Is he correct? Show both values.
123. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Inserting brackets can turn a false number sentence into a true one.
124. Sloan says $4 × 5²$ means $(4 × 5)²$. What is the correct value of $4 × 5²$?
125. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $a ÷ b × b$ equals $a$ when $b ≠ 0$.
126. Explain why $2 + 3 × 4 − 1$ gives 13, not 18 or 9.
127. **Always, sometimes, or never?** A calculation with no brackets has only one correct answer under BIDMAS.
128. Mi writes $20 − 5²$ as $(20 − 5)²$. Explain the error and give the correct value.
129. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $2(a + b)$ means the same as $2a + b$.
