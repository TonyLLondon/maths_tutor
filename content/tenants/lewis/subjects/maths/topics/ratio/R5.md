## Getting started

1. Share 10 sweets in the ratio 1 : 1. How many does each person get?
2. In the ratio 2 : 3, how many parts are there in total?
3. Share 20 in the ratio 1 : 1. What is each share?
4. In the ratio 3 : 2, which share is bigger — the first or the second?
5. Share 12 in the ratio 1 : 2. What is the smaller share?
6. A ratio is 4 : 1. How many parts are there in total?
7. One part of a ratio is worth £5. What is 3 parts worth?
8. Share 8 in the ratio 3 : 1. What is the larger share?

## Reasoning (AO2)
