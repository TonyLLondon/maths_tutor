## More reasoning (AO2)

93. **True or false?** Substituting $x = 1$ into $2x$ and into $x + x$ gives the same value. Show both.

94. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $x^2$ is positive when $x$ is a negative integer.

99. **True or false?** Substituting fractions can give a whole-number answer (try $x = \frac{1}{2}$ in $4x$).

105. **True or false?** $|x|$ and $x$ can give different values when $x$ is negative. Test with $x = -3$.
