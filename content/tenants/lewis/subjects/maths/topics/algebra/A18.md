## Problem (AO3)

31. A square tile has an area of 81 cm². Write and solve the equation $x^2 = 81$ to find the side length (take only the positive answer).

32. A rectangular garden has sides $(x + 2)$ m and $(x + 3)$ m. Its area is 30 m². Write and solve the equation $(x + 2)(x + 3) = 30$ to find $x$ (take only the positive answer).

33. The height, $h$, of a ball above the ground after $t$ seconds is given by $h = t(6 - t)$. At what values of $t$ is the ball at ground level ($h = 0$)?

34. The product of a number and 4 more than itself is 45. Write and solve an equation to find the positive value of the number.

35. A rectangle's length is 3 cm more than its width, $w$. Its area is 40 cm². Write and solve an equation for $w$ (take only the positive answer).

36. A number added to its own square gives 20. Write and solve an equation to find the positive value of the number.
