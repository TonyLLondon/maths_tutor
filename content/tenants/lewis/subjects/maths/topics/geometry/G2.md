## Getting started

1. Which tool helps you draw a straight line?
2. A 6 cm line is split into two equal parts. How long is each part?
3. What word means the exact middle of a line?
4. A 10 cm ribbon is folded exactly in half. How long is each half?
5. True or false? Two equal halves must be the same size.
6. Which tool draws accurate circles: compasses or a protractor?
7. An 8 cm line has its midpoint marked. How far is the midpoint from one end?
8. What angle is made by the corner of a square?

## Fluency (AO1)

9. What two tools do you need to construct an accurate perpendicular bisector of a line? Ruler and __________.
10. Fill in the blanks: A perpendicular bisector cuts a line segment exactly in __________, crossing it at __________°.
11. Draw a line segment 8 cm long and mark its midpoint. What is the distance from the midpoint to each end?
12. What is an "angle bisector"? It divides an angle into two __________ angles.
13. True or false? A locus is a path or region made up of all the points that follow one rule.
14. Write the missing word: the locus of points a fixed distance from one single point is a __________.
15. A dog is on a lead 3 m long, tied to a fixed post in an open field. Describe the shape of the region the dog can reach.
16. What is the locus of points that are the same distance from two fixed points A and B?
17. What is the locus of points that are the same distance from two straight lines that cross?
18. To bisect an angle with compasses, you draw an arc from the corner (vertex) that crosses __________ of the angle.
19. Every point on the perpendicular bisector of line AB is the same distance from A as it is from __________.
20. A circle of radius 5 cm is drawn around a point C. Every point on the circle is __________ cm from C.

## Reasoning (AO2)

24. Explain why the perpendicular bisector of a line segment always passes through its exact midpoint.

27. Sam bisects an angle of 80° using compasses. What size are the two new angles?

28. A line segment AB is 12 cm long. Points equidistant from A and B all lie on one line. Where does this line cross AB, and at what angle?

29. Two straight paths cross at a point in a park. A bench is to be placed so it is the same distance from both paths. Describe where the bench could be placed.

30. Leo constructs a right angle by bisecting a straight angle (a straight line, 180°). Explain why bisecting a straight angle always gives a right angle.

## Problem (AO3)

31. A radio mast broadcasts equally in every direction, reaching up to 15 km. Describe the shape of the region that can receive the signal, and state its radius.

32. Two phone masts, A and B, are 20 km apart. A phone always connects to whichever mast is nearer. Describe the line that separates "closer to A" from "closer to B".

33. A goat is tied to a post in the middle of a large field on a 5 m lead. Find the area it can graze over. Use area = π × radius² and round to 1 decimal place.

34. A ship's captain must stay at least 4 km from a straight coastline. Describe the locus of points that forms the edge of the safe zone.

35. A ladder needs to be placed so its foot is the same distance from two walls that meet at a right-angled corner. At what angle to each wall should the ladder's foot be positioned?

36. Archer's garden is a rectangle 6 m by 4 m. A sprinkler at the exact centre waters a circle of radius 3 m. The distance from the centre to each corner is √(3² + 2²) m. Does the water reach the corners?
