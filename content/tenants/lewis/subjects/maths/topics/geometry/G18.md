## Reasoning (AO2)

25. Ravi finds the area of a 180° sector by using the full circle formula $\pi r^2$ without adjusting for the angle. Explain his mistake.

26. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Doubling the radius of a sector (keeping the angle the same) doubles the arc length.

27. Priya says doubling the radius of a sector doubles its area. Explain why this is incorrect, and describe what actually happens.

28. A sector has angle 270°. Sloan says this means the sector is smaller than half the circle. Explain why she is wrong.

29. Explain why the formula for arc length uses $\frac{\theta}{360}$ multiplied by the full circumference, using the idea of a fraction of a full turn.

30. Leo says the perimeter of a sector is just the arc length. Explain what he has forgotten to include, and describe the full perimeter.
