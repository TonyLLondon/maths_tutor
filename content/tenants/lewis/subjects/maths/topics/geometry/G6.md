## Getting started

1. How many angles does a triangle have?
2. How many right angles does a square have?
3. A triangle has angles 60°, 60°, and 60°. Are all its angles equal?
4. An isosceles triangle has how many equal sides?
5. Which is greater: 70° or 40°?
6. A right angle is 90°. Find 90° − 20°.
7. Two equal angles are each 50°. What is their total?
8. A triangle has angles 90°, 45°, and 45°. Which two angles are equal?

## Fluency (AO1)

9. An isosceles triangle has two equal angles of 55° each. Find the third angle.
10. A triangle has one angle of 90° and another of 35°. Find the third angle.
11. On a straight line, one angle is 3x and the other is 2x. Find x, then find each angle.
12. A quadrilateral has three angles of 80°, 95°, and 100°. Find the fourth angle.
13. Two angles around a point are 145° and 110°. Find the third angle around that point.
14. An equilateral triangle has three equal sides. What are all three of its angles?
15. An isosceles triangle has a base angle of 72°. Find its apex (top) angle.
16. Two parallel lines are cut by a transversal, creating co-interior angles of 3x and x. Find x, then find each angle.
17. A right-angled triangle has one other angle of 28°. Find the third angle.
18. Vertically opposite angles are equal. If one is (2x + 10)° and the opposite one is 50°, find x.
19. Each interior angle of a square measures how many degrees?
20. In an isosceles triangle, the angles opposite the two equal sides are called __________ angles.


## More fluency

22. Can a triangle have sides 2 cm, 3 cm, and 7 cm?

25. A kite has two equal angles of 100° each, and one further angle of 45°. Find the fourth angle.

26. **Always, sometimes, or never?** In a triangle, the angle opposite the longest side is the largest angle.

27. A triangle has angles x, (x + 20)°, and (x + 40)°. Find x, then find all three angles.

28. Sam says, "The exterior angle of a triangle at one vertex is always 180° minus the interior angle at that same vertex." Is Sam correct? Explain, using angles on a straight line.

29. A parallelogram has one angle of x° and an adjacent angle of (2x + 30)°, which are co-interior angles (they add to 180°). Find x, then find both angles.

30. An isosceles triangle's apex angle is three times the size of one base angle. Find all three angles.
