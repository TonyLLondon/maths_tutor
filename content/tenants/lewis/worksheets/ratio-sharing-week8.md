## Fluency (AO1)

1. Share 20 counters in the ratio 1 : 1 (equal shares). How many each?
2. Share 15 in the ratio 2 : 1.
3. Simplify the ratio 6 : 9.

## Reasoning (AO2)

4. A map uses scale 1 cm : 5 km. Two stations are 4 cm apart on the map. How far apart are they in km? **Show your working.**

## Problem (AO3)

5. On a school trip in London, adult tickets and child tickets are bought in the ratio 2 : 5. There are 14 adult tickets. How many child tickets? How many tickets altogether?

## Stretch

6. Write your own word problem that uses the ratio 3 : 2 and a total of 25 items.
