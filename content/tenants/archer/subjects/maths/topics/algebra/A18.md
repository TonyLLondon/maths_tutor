## Reasoning (AO2)

23. Sam solves $x^2 = 16$ and writes $x = 4$ only. What has Sam missed? Give the full solution.

24. **Always, sometimes, or never?** A quadratic equation has two solutions.

25. Priya factorises $x^2 + 5x + 6$ as $(x + 2)(x + 3)$. Check her answer by expanding the brackets.

26. Two equations, $(x - 3)(x + 3) = 0$ and $x^2 = 9$, look different. Do they have the same solutions? Explain.

27. **True or false?** $x^2 - 9 = 0$ has the same solutions as $x^2 = 9$.

28. Leo factorises $x^2 - 4x + 3$ as $(x - 1)(x + 3)$. Explain Leo's mistake and give the correct factorisation.

29. Compare the solutions of $(x - 2)(x - 2) = 0$ and $(x - 2)(x + 2) = 0$.

30. Mo says every quadratic with a "+ number" at the end factorises into two brackets that both have a plus sign. Test his idea using $x^2 - 4x + 3$.
