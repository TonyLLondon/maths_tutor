## Fluency (AO1)

1. Solve $x^2 = 16$.
2. Solve $x^2 = 49$.
3. Solve $x^2 - 9 = 0$.
4. Factorise $x^2 + 5x + 6$.
5. Factorise $x^2 - 4x + 3$.
6. Solve $(x - 2)(x + 3) = 0$.
7. Solve $(x + 5)(x - 1) = 0$.
8. Factorise $x^2 + 7x + 12$.
9. Solve $x^2 + 5x + 6 = 0$.
10. Solve $x^2 - 4x + 3 = 0$.
11. Factorise $x^2 - 25$.
12. Solve $x^2 - 25 = 0$.

## Reasoning (AO2)

13. Sam solves $x^2 = 16$ and writes $x = 4$ only. What has Sam missed? Give the full solution.

14. **Always, sometimes, or never?** A quadratic equation has two solutions.

15. Priya factorises $x^2 + 5x + 6$ as $(x + 2)(x + 3)$. Check her answer by expanding the brackets.

16. Two equations, $(x - 3)(x + 3) = 0$ and $x^2 = 9$, look different. Do they have the same solutions? Explain.

17. **True or false?** $x^2 - 9 = 0$ has the same solutions as $x^2 = 9$.

18. Leo factorises $x^2 - 4x + 3$ as $(x - 1)(x + 3)$. Explain Leo's mistake and give the correct factorisation.

19. Compare the solutions of $(x - 2)(x - 2) = 0$ and $(x - 2)(x + 2) = 0$.

20. Mo says every quadratic with a "+ number" at the end factorises into two brackets that both have a plus sign. Test his idea using $x^2 - 4x + 3$.

## Problem (AO3)

21. A square tile has an area of 81 cm². Write and solve the equation $x^2 = 81$ to find the side length (take only the positive answer).

22. A rectangular garden has sides $(x + 2)$ m and $(x + 3)$ m. Its area is 30 m². Write and solve the equation $(x + 2)(x + 3) = 30$ to find $x$ (take only the positive answer).

23. The height, $h$, of a ball above the ground after $t$ seconds is given by $h = t(6 - t)$. At what values of $t$ is the ball at ground level ($h = 0$)?

24. The product of a number and 4 more than itself is 45. Write and solve an equation to find the positive value of the number.

25. A rectangle's length is 3 cm more than its width, $w$. Its area is 40 cm². Write and solve an equation for $w$ (take only the positive answer).

26. A number added to its own square gives 20. Write and solve an equation to find the positive value of the number.

## Stretch

27. Solve $2x^2 - 8 = 0$.

28. Solve $x^2 + 2x - 15 = 0$ by factorising, showing your full working.

29. A rectangle has length $x$ cm and width $(x - 4)$ cm. Its area is 45 cm². Find $x$, showing your working, and give the width.

30. Design your own quadratic word problem whose equation factorises to give a whole-number answer. Show the equation and its solution.
