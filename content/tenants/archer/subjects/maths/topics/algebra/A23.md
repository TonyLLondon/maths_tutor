## Fluency (AO1)

1. Write the next three terms: 4, 7, 10, 13, …
2. A sequence starts 20, 17, 14, 11, … What is the 6th term?
3. Complete the table.

   | Term number | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
   |---|---|---|---|---|
   | Term value  | 5 | 8 | 11 | ? |

4. The rule is “start at 3, add 6 each time”. Write the first four terms.

## Reasoning (AO2)

5. Sam says the sequence 2, 4, 8, 16, … adds 2 each time. What is wrong with Sam’s rule? Write the correct term-to-term rule.

6. Two sequences start at 100 and go down. Sequence A subtracts 7 each time; Sequence B subtracts 10. After 5 more terms (not counting the start), which sequence has the smaller value? **Show your working.**

## Problem (AO3)

7. In a board game, your score after each turn is 5, 12, 19, 26, … (same add-on rule each turn). What score will you have after turn 8?

## Stretch

8. A sequence follows an add-or-subtract rule from a table. The first four terms are 1, 4, 7, 10. Write the rule in words and find the 10th term.
