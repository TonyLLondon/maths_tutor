## Fluency (AO1)

1. Complete the table for $y = x + 2$.

   | $x$ | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 |
   |---|---|---|---|---|
   | $y$ |   |   |   |   |

2. Complete the table for $y = 3x - 1$.

   | $x$ | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 |
   |---|---|---|---|---|
   | $y$ |   |   |   |   |

3. Every point on the line $y = 4$ has the same $y$-value. What is $y$ when $x = 10$?
4. Every point on the line $x = 3$ has the same $x$-value. What is $x$ when $y = 100$?
5. Find where the line $y = x + 2$ crosses the $y$-axis (where $x = 0$).
6. Find where the line $y = 2x - 6$ crosses the $x$-axis (where $y = 0$).
7. Is the line $y = 5$ horizontal or vertical?
8. Is the line $x = -2$ horizontal or vertical?
9. Does the point $(2, 5)$ lie on the line $y = x + 3$? Show your check.
10. Does the point $(4, 9)$ lie on the line $y = 2x + 1$? Show your check.
11. Find the midpoint of $(0, 0)$ and $(6, 8)$.
12. Find the midpoint of $(1, 1)$ and $(5, 9)$.

## Reasoning (AO2)

13. Priya says the line $y = 3$ passes through the point $(7, 3)$. Is she correct? Explain using the rule for a horizontal line.

14. **Always, sometimes, or never?** A straight line with equation $y = x + c$ crosses the $y$-axis at the point $(0, c)$.

15. Sam checks whether $(3, 10)$ lies on $y = 3x + 1$ by substituting $x = 3$. Show the check, and say whether Sam's point is on the line.

16. Two lines, $x = 4$ and $y = 4$, are drawn on the same grid. Describe where they cross, and explain how you know without plotting every point.

17. **True or false?** Every point on the line $y = 2x$ has a $y$-value that is exactly double its $x$-value.

18. Leo says the line $y = x - 5$ crosses the $x$-axis at $(0, -5)$. Explain Leo's mistake, and find the correct crossing point.

19. Compare the lines $y = x + 1$ and $y = x - 1$. Do they cross the $y$-axis at the same point? Explain.

20. Mo says $(2, 2)$, $(4, 4)$, and $(6, 6)$ all lie on the same straight line. Write the equation of that line and explain how you know.

## Problem (AO3)

21. A cycle hire scheme's cost line passes through $(0, 1)$ and $(1, 1.5)$. Write the coordinates of the point when 2 units of time have passed, assuming the pattern continues by the same amount each time.

22. A delivery charge line is given by $y = 5$ for any number of items, $x$. Explain in words what this tells you about the delivery charge.

23. A line representing a car's fixed parking fee is $x = 2$ hours. Explain why this is a vertical line rather than a sloped one.

24. A straight-line graph for cost against months is $y = 20 + 5x$. Find where this line crosses the $y$-axis, and explain what that point means in the context.

25. Two friends' savings are modelled by $y = 2x + 3$ and $y = 3x$. Find the point where both lines have $y = 9$, and state the value of $x$ for each equation.

26. A straight line joins $(0, 4)$ and $(4, 12)$. Find the midpoint of these two points, and check whether the midpoint also lies on the line $y = 2x + 4$.

## Stretch

27. A line passes through $(1, 4)$ and $(3, 8)$. Find a third point that lies on the same line, showing how you worked it out.

28. Find the coordinates of the points where the line $y = 2x - 8$ crosses both axes.

29. A line has equation $y = x + c$ and passes through $(3, 10)$. Find the value of $c$.

30. Show that the points $(1, 3)$, $(2, 5)$, and $(4, 9)$ all lie on the line $y = 2x + 1$.
