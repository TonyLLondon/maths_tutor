## Fluency (AO1)

1. Find the nth term of the sequence 3, 5, 7, 9, …
2. Find the nth term of the sequence 4, 7, 10, 13, …
3. Find the nth term of the sequence 10, 8, 6, 4, …
4. Find the nth term of the sequence 5, 9, 13, 17, …
5. Using the nth term rule $3n + 1$, find the first four terms.
6. Using the nth term rule $2n - 5$, find the first four terms.
7. Find the nth term of the sequence 1, 4, 7, 10, …
8. Find the nth term of the sequence 20, 17, 14, 11, …
9. Using the nth term rule $n + 6$, find the 10th term.
10. Using the nth term rule $5n - 2$, find the 20th term.
11. Find the nth term of the sequence 6, 10, 14, 18, …
12. Using the nth term rule $4n + 3$, find the 15th term.

## Reasoning (AO2)

13. Sam finds the nth term of 2, 5, 8, 11, … and writes $3n$. Explain Sam's mistake and find the correct rule.

14. **Always, sometimes, or never?** The nth term of a linear sequence gives a positive value.

15. Priya says the nth term $2n + 1$ will always give an odd number, whatever whole number $n$ is. Is she correct? Explain.

16. Two sequences have nth terms $3n + 2$ and $3n - 1$. Explain why they have the same common difference but different starting values.

17. **True or false?** If the nth term rule contains "$-n$", like $10 - 2n$, the sequence is decreasing.

18. Leo finds the nth term of 7, 10, 13, 16, … and writes $3n + 3$. Check Leo's rule against the first two terms of the sequence — is he correct?

19. Compare the nth term rules $5n$ and $5n + 2$. Which sequence's terms are bigger for the same value of $n$, and by how much?

20. Mo says 50 is a term in the sequence with nth term $3n + 2$. Is Mo correct? Show your working.

## Problem (AO3)

21. A pattern of matchstick squares uses $3n + 1$ matches for pattern number $n$. How many matches are needed for pattern 12?

22. A theatre charges a £5 booking fee plus £8 per ticket. Write the nth term rule for the total cost of $n$ tickets, then find the cost of 6 tickets.

23. Archer saves money according to the rule $4n + 10$ pounds after $n$ weeks. How much has he saved after 8 weeks?

24. A sequence of tile patterns has nth term $2n + 3$. Which pattern number has exactly 25 tiles?

25. A café stacks chairs so that the nth stack has $5n - 3$ chairs. How many chairs are in the 6th stack, and what is the term-to-term rule for this sequence?

26. Is 100 a term in the sequence with nth term $6n - 2$? Show your working to justify your answer.

## Stretch

27. Find the nth term of the sequence 100, 93, 86, 79, … and use it to find the first negative term.

28. A sequence has nth term $7n - 4$. Find the value of $n$ for which the term equals 108.

29. Two sequences have nth terms $4n + 1$ and $2n + 15$. Find the value of $n$ for which both sequences give the same term.

30. Design your own linear sequence whose nth term rule includes a negative coefficient of $n$, such as $-2n$ or $10 - 3n$. Write the rule and give its first four terms.
