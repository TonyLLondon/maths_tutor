## Fluency (AO1)

1. The formula for perimeter of a square is $P = 4s$. Find $P$ when $s = 6$.
2. The formula for area of a rectangle is $A = lw$. Find $A$ when $l = 5$ and $w = 3$.
3. Rearrange $P = 4s$ to make $s$ the subject.
4. Rearrange $y = x + 3$ to make $x$ the subject.
5. Rearrange $y = x - 5$ to make $x$ the subject.
6. Rearrange $y = 2x$ to make $x$ the subject.
7. Rearrange $A = lw$ to make $l$ the subject.
8. The formula for a rectangle's perimeter is $P = 2l + 2w$. Find $P$ when $l = 7$ and $w = 4$.
9. Rearrange $y = 3x + 1$ to make $x$ the subject.
10. Rearrange $y = 5x - 2$ to make $x$ the subject.
11. The formula $C = 5 + 2n$ gives the cost, $C$, of $n$ items. Find $C$ when $n = 8$.
12. Rearrange $v = u + t$ to make $u$ the subject.

## Reasoning (AO2)

13. Sam rearranges $P = 4s$ and writes $s = P - 4$. Explain Sam's mistake and give the correct rearrangement.

14. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Rearranging a formula changes which letter you can calculate directly, but not the relationship between the letters.

15. Priya rearranges $y = x + 3$ to get $x = y + 3$. Explain what Priya did wrong.

16. Two students rearrange $A = lw$ for $w$. One writes $w = \dfrac{A}{l}$, the other writes $w = A - l$. Which is correct? Explain how you know, using $A = 20$ and $l = 4$.

17. **True or false?** If $y = 2x + 5$, then subtracting 5 from both sides and dividing by 2 gives $x$ in terms of $y$.

18. Leo rearranges $y = 3x - 1$ and writes $x = \dfrac{y-1}{3}$, but Sam says it should be $x = \dfrac{y+1}{3}$. Who is correct? Show your working.

19. Compare substituting a value into a formula with rearranging a formula. Explain when you would use each approach.

20. Mo rearranges $C = 5 + 2n$ for $n$ and gets $n = \dfrac{C+5}{2}$. Explain Mo's mistake and find the correct rearrangement.

## Problem (AO3)

21. The formula for converting kilometres, $k$, to miles, $m$, is $m = 0.6k$. Rearrange to make $k$ the subject, then find $k$ when $m = 30$.

22. A taxi's cost is $C = 3 + 2m$ for $m$ miles. Rearrange to make $m$ the subject, then find $m$ when $C = 17$.

23. The formula for the perimeter of a rectangle is $P = 2l + 2w$. Rearrange to make $l$ the subject, then find $l$ when $P = 24$ and $w = 5$.

24. A recipe formula gives the cooking time in minutes as $T = 20 + 30w$, where $w$ is the weight in kg. Rearrange to make $w$ the subject, then find $w$ when $T = 110$.

25. The formula for converting Celsius, $c$, to a rough Fahrenheit estimate is $f = 2c + 30$. Rearrange to make $c$ the subject, then find $c$ when $f = 70$.

26. A phone plan costs $C = 10 + 0.05t$ pounds for $t$ texts. Rearrange to make $t$ the subject, then find $t$ when $C = 15$.

## Stretch

27. Rearrange $y = \dfrac{x}{2} + 4$ to make $x$ the subject.

28. Rearrange $A = \dfrac{1}{2}bh$ to make $h$ the subject.

29. Rearrange $2y = 3x - 6$ to make $x$ the subject.

30. The formula for the volume of a cuboid is $V = lwh$. Rearrange to make $h$ the subject, then find $h$ when $V = 60$, $l = 5$, and $w = 4$.
