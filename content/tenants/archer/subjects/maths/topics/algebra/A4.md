## Fluency (AO1)

1. Simplify $3x + 2x$.
2. Simplify $5y - 2y$.
3. Simplify $4x + 3 + 2x$.
4. Simplify $6a - 2a + a$.
5. Simplify $3x + 2y + x + y$.
6. Simplify $5a + 3b - 2a - b$.
7. Expand $2(x + 3)$.
8. Expand $3(x - 4)$.
9. Expand $5(2x + 1)$.
10. Expand $4(3x - 2)$.
11. Simplify $x \times x$.
12. Simplify $3x \times 2$.

## Reasoning (AO2)

13. Sam simplifies $3x + 2x$ and gets $5x^2$. Explain Sam's mistake and give the correct answer.

14. **Always, sometimes, or never?** $2(x + 5)$ gives the same result as $2x + 5$.

15. Priya expands $4(x - 3)$ and writes $4x - 3$. Explain what Priya forgot to do, and give the correct expansion.

16. Two expressions, $3(x + 2)$ and $3x + 6$, are claimed to be equivalent. Test this by substituting $x = 4$ into both.

17. **True or false?** You can only collect terms with $x$ in them; terms with $y$ must stay separate. Use $3x + 2y + x$ to support your answer.

18. Leo simplifies $5x - 3x + 2x$ and gets $0x$. Explain Leo's mistake and find the correct simplified expression.

19. Compare simplifying $4x + 3x$ with expanding $4(x + 3)$. Why do these give different types of answers?

20. Mo says $2(x + y)$ expands to $2x + y$. Explain Mo's error and expand it correctly.

## Problem (AO3)

21. A rectangle has width $x$ and length $x + 3$. Write and simplify an expression for its perimeter.

22. Archer buys $x$ pens at 20p each and $x$ pencils at 15p each. Write and simplify an expression for the total cost in pence.

23. A garden has two rectangular beds. One has area $3(x + 2)$ and the other has area $2x$. Expand and simplify an expression for the total area.

24. A shop sells $t$-shirts for £($x + 5$) and shorts for £$x$. Sloan buys 2 t-shirts and 3 pairs of shorts. Write and simplify an expression for the total cost.

25. A triangle has sides $2x$, $x + 3$, and $x + 3$. Write and simplify an expression for its perimeter.

26. A pack of $n$ stickers costs $5(n + 1)$ pence including a fixed handling charge. Expand this expression and explain what the numbers mean.

## Stretch

27. Simplify $3(x + 2) + 2(x - 1)$.

28. Simplify $5(2x - 1) - 3(x + 2)$.

29. Show that $4(x + 3) - 2x$ simplifies to $2x + 12$. Show each step.

30. A rectangle's perimeter is $2(x + 4) + 2(x + 4)$. Simplify this fully, then explain what type of shape this perimeter expression suggests.
