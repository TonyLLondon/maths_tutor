## Fluency (AO1)

1. Write an expression for "a number, $n$, plus 5".
2. Write an expression for "3 times a number, $x$".
3. Write an expression for "a number, $y$, take away 4".
4. In the expression $3x + 2$, what is the coefficient of $x$?
5. In the expression $5x - 7$, what is the constant term?
6. How many terms are in the expression $2x + 3y - 1$?
7. Is $2x + 3 = 9$ an **expression** or an **equation**?
8. Is $3x + 6$ an **expression** or an **equation**?
9. Write an expression for "double a number, $t$, then add 3".
10. Write an expression for the perimeter of a square with side length $s$.
11. Write an equation that says "a number, $x$, plus 7 equals 15".
12. Simplify $x + x + x$ by writing it as a single term.

## Reasoning (AO2)

13. Priya says $2x + 3$ is an equation because it has an $x$ in it. Explain why Priya is wrong.

14. **Always, sometimes, or never?** An expression can be solved to find a single value of $x$.

15. Sam writes the identity $2(x + 3) \equiv 2x + 6$. Explain what the $\equiv$ symbol means and why it is used here instead of $=$.

16. Explain the difference between an **expression**, an **equation**, and a **formula**, using one example of each.

17. **True or false?** $x + x + x = 3x$ is true for every possible value of $x$, so it could be written as an identity.

18. Leo says the perimeter formula $P = 4s$ for a square is an equation. Explain why it is better described as a **formula**.

19. Compare $5x + 2$ and $5x + 2 = 17$. What can you find from the second one that you cannot find from the first?

20. Mo says "an equation always has an equals sign, so $2x + 3$ must be an equation." What is wrong with Mo's reasoning?

## Problem (AO3)

21. Archer is $x$ years old. Sloan is 3 years older. Write an expression for Sloan's age.

22. A rectangle has width $w$ and length twice the width. Write an expression for the length.

23. A taxi charges a £3 flat fee plus £2 per mile, $m$. Write an expression for the total cost.

24. A packet of stickers costs $p$ pence. Write an expression for the cost of 5 packets.

25. Sloan has $n$ marbles. Archer has 4 fewer marbles than twice Sloan's amount. Write an expression for Archer's marbles.

26. The total age of three siblings is 27. The youngest is $x$, the middle one is $x + 2$, and the eldest is $x + 5$. Write and simplify an expression for the total age in terms of $x$.

## Stretch

27. Write an expression for "the square of a number, $x$, plus twice the number".

28. Show that $3(x + 2) + 1$ and $3x + 7$ are equivalent expressions by expanding the first one.

29. A gardener charges a call-out fee of £$c$ plus £$r$ for every hour worked. Write a formula for the total charge, $T$, for $h$ hours of work.

30. Explain, using your own examples, why $2x + 3$ is an expression, $2x + 3 = 9$ is an equation, and $2(x + 3) \equiv 2x + 6$ is an identity.
