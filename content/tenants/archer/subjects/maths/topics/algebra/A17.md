## Fluency (AO1)

1. Solve $x + 5 = 12$.
2. Solve $x - 3 = 9$.
3. Solve $3x = 21$.
4. Solve $\dfrac{x}{4} = 5$.
5. Solve $2x + 3 = 11$.
6. Solve $3x - 4 = 14$.
7. Solve $5x + 2 = 27$.
8. Solve $\dfrac{x}{3} + 1 = 6$.
9. Solve $2(x + 3) = 16$.
10. Solve $4(x - 1) = 20$.
11. Solve $3x + 5 = 2x + 9$.
12. Solve $5x - 2 = 3x + 8$.

## Reasoning (AO2)

13. Sam solves $2x + 4 = 12$ and writes $x = 8$. What did Sam do wrong? Find the correct value of $x$.

14. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Multiplying both sides of an equation by the same number keeps the equation balanced.

15. Priya says the equation $3x = x + 8$ has no solution because $x$ appears on both sides. Is she correct? Solve the equation to check.

16. Two equations, $2x + 1 = 9$ and $3x - 2 = 10$, look different. Do they have the same solution? **Show your working.**

17. **True or false?** If you add the same number to both sides of an equation, the solution doesn't change. Give an example to support your answer.

18. Leo solves $5(x - 2) = 15$ by writing $5x - 2 = 15$. Explain Leo's mistake, then solve the equation correctly.

19. Compare the solutions of $x + 6 = 10$ and $2x + 6 = 10$. Explain why the two solutions are different.

20. Mo says $x = 4$ is the solution of both $2x = 8$ and $3x - 2 = 13$. Check whether Mo is correct for **both** equations.

## Problem (AO3)

21. Archer's age plus 5 is 14. Write an equation for Archer's age and solve it.

22. A taxi charges a £3 fixed fee plus £2 per mile. The total fare was £13. Write and solve an equation to find the number of miles.

23. Three times a number, plus 7, is 28. Find the number.

24. Sloan buys 4 identical pens and a £2 ruler, spending £10 in total. Write and solve an equation for the cost of one pen.

25. The perimeter of a rectangle is 26 cm. The length is 3 cm more than the width, $w$. Write and solve an equation for $w$.

26. A gym charges a £15 joining fee plus £10 per month. Archer's total bill so far is £75. Write and solve an equation to find the number of months.

## Stretch

27. Solve $2(x + 3) = 3(x - 1)$.

28. Solve $\dfrac{x}{2} + \dfrac{x}{3} = 5$.

29. The sum of three consecutive integers is 72. Write and solve an equation to find the three integers.

30. Design your own linear equation, using at least two steps to solve, whose solution is $x = 6$. Write your equation and show that it solves to give $x = 6$.
