## Getting started

1. Share 8 strawberries equally between 2 children. How many does each child get?
2. What fraction of 6 counters is 3 counters?
3. A bag has 2 red and 2 blue balls. What fraction are red?
4. There are 12 cakes. One quarter are chocolate. How many are chocolate?
5. Share 15 stickers in the ratio 1:2. How many stickers are in the smaller share?
6. A box has 3 cats and 1 dog toy. What fraction of the toys are cat toys?
7. There are 10 apples. 2 are green. What fraction are green?
8. Mia gives half of 14 pencils away. How many pencils does she keep?
9. Share 10 sweets equally between 2 children. How many does each child get?
10. What fraction of 8 is 4?
11. A tin has 3 red and 3 blue counters. What fraction of the counters are red?
12. Half of 20 is ___.

## Fluency (AO1)

13. Share £40 in the ratio 1:3. Write the smaller share as a fraction of the total amount.
14. In a class of 30 pupils, the ratio of boys to girls is 2:3. What fraction of the class are girls?
15. A recipe uses flour and sugar in the ratio 5:2. What fraction of the mixture is sugar?
16. Share 24 sweets in the ratio 1:2. Write the larger share as a fraction of the total.
17. The ratio of red to blue counters in a bag is 3:7. What fraction of the counters are red?
18. Share £60 in the ratio 2:3:5. Write the middle share as a fraction of the total amount.
19. The ratio of cats to dogs is 4:1. What fraction of the animals are dogs?
20. Share 36 marbles in the ratio 1:1:4. Write the smallest share as a fraction of the total.
21. In a bag of counters, the ratio of green to yellow is 5:3. What fraction of the counters are yellow?
22. The ratio of adults to children on a coach is 3:5. What fraction of the passengers are adults?

## More fluency

23. Share £90 in the ratio 4:5. Work out the smaller share.
24. In a fruit bowl, the ratio of apples to oranges is 2:5. If there are 14 pieces of fruit in total, how many are apples?
25. A ratio is 1:4. Write this ratio as two fractions of the whole.
26. The ratio of Archer's stickers to Sloan's stickers is 3:4. What fraction of the total stickers does Archer have?

## Reasoning (AO2)

27. Archer says a ratio of 1:3 means the first part is $\dfrac{1}{3}$ of the total. Is he correct? Explain the mistake and give the correct fraction.

28. **Always, sometimes, or never?** In a ratio $a:b$, the first part is $\dfrac{a}{a+b}$ of the total.

29. Sloan says a ratio of 2:2 means each part is a different fraction of the whole. Is she correct? Explain.

30. **Always, sometimes, or never?** If a ratio is 1:1, both fractions of the whole are equal.

31. Compare the ratio 3:5 written as fractions of the whole with the ratio 5:3. Explain why the fractions swap around.

## Problem (AO3)

32. A charity raises £500 split between two projects in the ratio 3:2. What fraction of the money goes to the larger project, and how much money is that?

33. In a school of 400 pupils, the ratio of those who walk to school to those who don't is 3:5. How many pupils walk to school?

34. A paint mix uses blue and white paint in the ratio 1:4. What fraction of a 20-litre tin should be blue paint, and how many litres is that?

35. Archer and Sloan share a £45 prize in the ratio 4:5. What fraction of the prize does Sloan get, and how much money is that?

36. A fruit drink is made from orange juice and water in the ratio 2:7. What fraction of a 900 ml bottle is orange juice, and how many ml is that?

## Stretch

37. A garden is divided into flowerbeds and lawn in the ratio 2:3. If the flowerbeds cover 24 m², what is the total area of the garden?

38. A class has boys and girls in the ratio 3:4. If there are 12 more girls than boys, how many pupils are in the class?

39. A recipe uses three ingredients in the ratio 2:3:5. What fraction of the mixture is made up of the two smaller ingredients combined?

40. A sum of money is shared in the ratio 1:2:3 between Archer, Sloan, and their cousin. If Archer receives £15, what fraction of the total does the cousin receive, and how much money is that?

## Mixed practice

41. The ratio of cats to dogs at a rescue centre is 5:3. Write the fraction of animals that are dogs, then work out how many dogs there are if there are 64 animals in total.

42. Sloan says if a ratio is 7:1, the second part must be a very small fraction of the total. Work out the exact fraction to check if she is correct.
