## Fluency (AO1)

1. Share £40 in the ratio 1:3. Write the smaller share as a fraction of the total amount.
2. In a class of 30 pupils, the ratio of boys to girls is 2:3. What fraction of the class are girls?
3. A recipe uses flour and sugar in the ratio 5:2. What fraction of the mixture is sugar?
4. Share 24 sweets in the ratio 1:2. Write the larger share as a fraction of the total.
5. The ratio of red to blue counters in a bag is 3:7. What fraction of the counters are red?
6. Share £60 in the ratio 2:3:5. Write the middle share as a fraction of the total amount.
7. The ratio of cats to dogs is 4:1. What fraction of the animals are dogs?
8. Share 36 marbles in the ratio 1:1:4. Write the smallest share as a fraction of the total.
9. In a bag of counters, the ratio of green to yellow is 5:3. What fraction of the counters are yellow?
10. The ratio of adults to children on a coach is 3:5. What fraction of the passengers are adults?

## More fluency

11. Share £90 in the ratio 4:5. Work out the smaller share.
12. In a fruit bowl, the ratio of apples to oranges is 2:5. If there are 14 pieces of fruit in total, how many are apples?
13. A ratio is 1:4. Write this ratio as two fractions of the whole.
14. The ratio of Archer's stickers to Sloan's stickers is 3:4. What fraction of the total stickers does Archer have?

## Reasoning (AO2)

15. Archer says a ratio of 1:3 means the first part is $\dfrac{1}{3}$ of the total. Is he correct? Explain the mistake and give the correct fraction.

16. **Always, sometimes, or never?** In a ratio $a:b$, the first part is $\dfrac{a}{a+b}$ of the total.

17. Sloan says a ratio of 2:2 means each part is a different fraction of the whole. Is she correct? Explain.

18. **Always, sometimes, or never?** If a ratio is 1:1, both fractions of the whole are equal.

19. Compare the ratio 3:5 written as fractions of the whole with the ratio 5:3. Explain why the fractions swap around.

## Problem (AO3)

20. A charity raises £500 split between two projects in the ratio 3:2. What fraction of the money goes to the larger project, and how much money is that?

21. In a school of 400 pupils, the ratio of those who walk to school to those who don't is 3:5. How many pupils walk to school?

22. A paint mix uses blue and white paint in the ratio 1:4. What fraction of a 20-litre tin should be blue paint, and how many litres is that?

23. Archer and Sloan share a £45 prize in the ratio 4:5. What fraction of the prize does Sloan get, and how much money is that?

24. A fruit drink is made from orange juice and water in the ratio 2:7. What fraction of a 900 ml bottle is orange juice, and how many ml is that?

## Stretch

25. A garden is divided into flowerbeds and lawn in the ratio 2:3. If the flowerbeds cover 24 m², what is the total area of the garden?

26. A class has boys and girls in the ratio 3:4. If there are 12 more girls than boys, how many pupils are in the class?

27. A recipe uses three ingredients in the ratio 2:3:5. What fraction of the mixture is made up of the two smaller ingredients combined?

28. A sum of money is shared in the ratio 1:2:3 between Archer, Sloan, and their cousin. If Archer receives £15, what fraction of the total does the cousin receive, and how much money is that?

## Mixed practice

29. The ratio of cats to dogs at a rescue centre is 5:3. Write the fraction of animals that are dogs, then work out how many dogs there are if there are 64 animals in total.

30. Sloan says if a ratio is 7:1, the second part must be a very small fraction of the total. Work out the exact fraction to check if she is correct.
