## Fluency (AO1)

1. Work out 18 − 3 × 4.
2. Work out 24 ÷ (2 + 2).
3. Work out 5 + 2² × 3.
4. Insert brackets to make a true statement: 3 + 4 × 5 = 35.

## Reasoning (AO2)

5. Priya says 10 − 6 ÷ 2 is the same as (10 − 6) ÷ 2. Is she correct? Show the value of each expression.

6. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Brackets change the answer of a calculation. Give one example where they do and one where they do not (if possible).

## Problem (AO3)

7. A Tube day pass costs £8.50. Archer buys 3 passes and pays with a £30 note. He uses the rule “multiply first, then subtract”: cost = 3 × 8.50, then change = 30 − cost. Work out his change.

## Stretch

8. Work out 100 − 4 × (3 + 2)².
