## Getting started

1. Work out 2 + 3 × 2 by doing the multiplication first.
2. Work out (2 + 3) × 2.
3. What operation undoes adding 4?
4. What operation undoes doubling?
5. Fill the box: 5 + □ = 9.
6. Fill the box: 3 × □ = 12.
7. Start with 7, add 5, then subtract 5. What number do you finish with?
8. Work out 10 − (3 + 2).

## Fluency (AO1)

9. Work out 18 − 3 × 4.
10. Work out 24 ÷ (2 + 2).
11. Work out 5 + 2² × 3.
12. Insert brackets to make a true statement: 3 + 4 × 5 = 35.
13. Work out 6 + 3².
14. Work out (6 + 3)².
15. Work out 20 − 2 × (3 + 1).
16. Work out 12 ÷ 3 + 2 × 5.
17. Work out 3³ − 10.
18. Work out (10 − 4) ÷ 3 + 1.

## More fluency

19. Work out 7 + 6 ÷ 2 − 1.
20. Work out 2 × (5 + 3²).
21. Insert brackets to make this correct: 2 + 3 × 4 − 1 = 19.
22. Work out 100 ÷ (5² − 5).

## Reasoning (AO2)

23. Priya says 10 − 6 ÷ 2 is the same as (10 − 6) ÷ 2. Is she correct? Show the value of each expression.

24. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Brackets change the answer of a calculation. Give one example where they do and one where they do not (if possible).

25. Archer says squaring a number always makes it bigger. Test his idea with a number between 0 and 1. Is he correct?

26. **Always, sometimes, or never?** For a calculation like *a* + *b* × *c*, working left to right (add, then multiply) gives the same answer as multiplying first. Give an example to support your answer.

27. Sloan calculates 4 + 5 × 2 and gets 18 by working left to right. Explain her mistake and give the correct answer.

## Problem (AO3)

28. A Tube day pass costs £8.50. Archer buys 3 passes and pays with a £30 note. He uses the rule "multiply first, then subtract": cost = 3 × 8.50, then change = 30 − cost. Work out his change.

29. A recipe needs 2 trays of 6 buns, then 3 more trays of 4 buns. Write one calculation using brackets to find the total number of buns, then work it out.

30. A garden is a square with side length (3 + 4) m. Work out its area.

31. A phone plan costs £15 a month plus £2 for every GB used over 5 GB. Sloan uses 8 GB in a month. Write a calculation for the total cost and work it out.

32. Work out 50 − (2 + 3)² ÷ 5.

## More practice

34. Work out (3 + 4) × 2.

37. Work out 10 − 2 × 3 + 1.

## Stretch

39. Work out 100 − 4 × (3 + 2)².

40. Insert brackets to make this true: 6 + 4 × 2 − 3 = 17.

41. Work out 3 + 2 × (4² − 3 × 5).

42. Work out (2 + 3)² − (4 − 1)³.

## Mixed practice

43. Work out 15 − 3 × (6 − 4)² ÷ 2.

44. Archer says (2 + 3)² is the same as 2² + 3². Is he correct? Show both values.
