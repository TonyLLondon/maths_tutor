## Fluency (AO1)

1. Round 487 to the nearest 10.
2. Round 6.28 to one decimal place.
3. Estimate 39 × 21 by rounding each number to the nearest 10.
4. Estimate 502 ÷ 9 by rounding to one significant figure (or nearest easy numbers).

## Reasoning (AO2)

5. A friend estimates 198 + 305 as 200 + 300 = 500. The exact answer is 503. Is the estimate sensible? Explain.

6. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Rounding both numbers up before multiplying gives an estimate that is bigger than the true answer. Give an example.

## Problem (AO3)

7. About 31 000 people attend a football match at Wembley. Tickets are roughly £48 each. By rounding sensibly, estimate the total ticket income. Show what you rounded to.

## Stretch

8. Work out 49 × 52 exactly. Then use rounding to estimate the product and compare your estimate with the exact value.
