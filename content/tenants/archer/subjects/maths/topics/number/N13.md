## Fluency (AO1)

1. Convert 3.5 km to metres.
2. Convert 250 cm to metres.
3. Convert 2 hours to minutes.
4. Convert 1500 g to kg.
5. A car travels 120 km in 2 hours. Work out its average speed in km/h.
6. Convert 4 litres to millilitres.
7. A cyclist travels 30 miles in 3 hours. Work out the average speed in mph.
8. Convert 0.75 kg to grams.
9. Work out the density of an object with mass 20 g and volume 4 cm³ (density = mass ÷ volume).
10. Convert 90 minutes to hours.

## More fluency

11. A runner covers 400 m in 50 seconds. Work out the average speed in m/s.
12. Convert 2.5 tonnes to kg (1 tonne = 1000 kg).
13. Work out the mass of an object with density 5 g/cm³ and volume 8 cm³ (mass = density × volume).
14. Convert 5000 m to km.

## Reasoning (AO2)

15. Archer says speed only needs one unit, like "metres". Explain why speed needs two units, and give an example.

16. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Converting from a smaller unit to a larger unit means dividing.

17. Sloan says 1.5 hours is the same as 1 hour 5 minutes. Explain her mistake and give the correct number of minutes.

18. **Always, sometimes, or never?** Density = mass ÷ volume.

19. Compare a car travelling at 60 km/h and a cyclist travelling at 20 m/s. Convert both to the same units to say which is faster.

## Problem (AO3)

20. A train travels 180 km in 1.5 hours. Work out its average speed in km/h.

21. Archer walks 2 km in 25 minutes. Work out his average speed in km/h.

22. A metal block has a mass of 540 g and a volume of 60 cm³. Work out its density.

23. A tank holds 15,000 ml of water. How many litres is this?

24. A cyclist rides at 24 km/h for 45 minutes. How far does she travel?

## Stretch

25. A car travels at an average speed of 90 km/h for 2 hours 30 minutes. How far does it travel?

26. A block of wood has a volume of 200 cm³ and a density of 0.8 g/cm³. Work out its mass.

27. Two runners: Runner A covers 100 m in 12 seconds; Runner B covers 400 m in 52 seconds. Work out each speed in m/s and say who is faster.

28. A lorry travels 350 km at an average speed of 70 km/h. How long does the journey take, in hours and minutes?

## Mixed practice

29. Convert 45 km/h into m/s (1 km = 1000 m, 1 hour = 3600 s).

30. Sloan says a speed of 5 m/s is faster than 15 km/h. Convert both to the same units to check if she is correct.
