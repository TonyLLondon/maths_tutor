## Fluency (AO1)

1. List all the factors of 36.
2. List the first six multiples of 8.
3. Circle the prime numbers: 8, 13, 19, 21, 29.
4. Work out the highest common factor (HCF) of 20 and 30.

## Reasoning (AO2)

5. Jess says 1 is a prime number because it only has one factor. Is Jess correct? Explain.

6. **Always, sometimes, or never?** The lowest common multiple (LCM) of two numbers is one of the numbers. Give an example for each answer you choose.

## Problem (AO3)

7. A youth club on the Isle of Dogs is packing identical welcome packs for a trip. They have 48 water bottles and 72 snack bars. Each pack must have the same number of bottles and bars, with none left over. What is the greatest number of packs they can make? How many bottles and bars in each pack?

## Stretch

8. Write 60 as a product of its prime factors.
