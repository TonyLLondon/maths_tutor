## Fluency (AO1)

1. Work out **1/2** of 84.
2. Work out **3/4** of 20.
3. Work out 10% of £60.
4. Work out 50% of 146.

## Reasoning (AO2)

5. A shop label says “10% off £40”. Layla says the sale price is £4. What mistake has Layla made?

6. **Always, sometimes, or never?** To find 25% of a number you can divide by 4. Explain with an example.

## Problem (AO3)

7. A Camden market stall sells juice for £2.50 a cup. Archer buys 4 cups and pays with a £20 note. He is told he gets 10% off the total. How much change should he receive?

## Stretch

8. Work out 20% of £45 without a calculator. Then work out **2/5** of £45. What do you notice?
