## Fluency (AO1)

1. Write these integers in order from smallest to largest: −4, 2, 0, −1, 3.
2. Write these decimals in order from smallest to largest: 0.6, 0.06, 0.606, 0.66.
3. Which is larger: **3/4** or **7/10**? Show how you know.
4. Fill in the correct symbol (< , > , or =): −5 ___ −2.

## Reasoning (AO2)

5. On a number line, −3 is to the left of 1. Where would −3.5 go compared with −3? Explain in one sentence.

6. **Always, sometimes, or never?** A negative number is less than any positive number. Give a counterexample if you think it is *never* true.

## Problem (AO3)

7. At 8 a.m. the temperature at Greenwich Park was −2 °C. By midday it had risen by 5 °C. At 6 p.m. it had fallen by 7 °C from midday. What was the temperature at 6 p.m.?

## Stretch

8. Write these in order from smallest to largest: **1/2**, 0.45, 40%, **2/5**.
