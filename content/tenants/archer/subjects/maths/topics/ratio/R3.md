## Getting started

1. Write one half as a fraction.
2. There is 1 red counter and 2 blue counters. The number of red counters is what fraction of the number of blue counters?
3. Write the ratio 1 : 2 as a fraction comparing the first amount with the second.
4. Which is larger: 1/3 or 1/2?
5. A ribbon is 2 m long and another is 4 m long. The shorter is what fraction of the longer?
6. Write the fraction 2/3 as a ratio.
7. Write 4 as a fraction of 8, in simplest form.
8. A book costs £3 and a game costs £6. The book's cost is what fraction of the game's cost?
9. Write 3 as a fraction of 9, in simplest form.
10. Which is larger: 1/4 or 2/5?
11. Write the ratio 6 : 9 in simplest form.
12. A ribbon is 3 m long and another is 9 m long. The shorter ribbon is what fraction of the longer?

## Fluency (AO1)

13. Write the ratio 3 : 5 as a fraction: "the first amount is ___ of the second amount."
14. There are 8 red counters and 4 blue counters. Write the number of red counters as a fraction of the number of blue counters.
15. A rope is 6 m long and another rope is 15 m long. Write the length of the shorter rope as a fraction of the longer rope.
16. A bag has 10 apples and 4 pears. Write the number of pears as a fraction of the number of apples.
17. A small cake weighs 200 g and a large cake weighs 800 g. Write the weight of the small cake as a fraction of the large cake.
18. Write the fraction 3/4 as a ratio.
19. Write the ratio 5 : 2 as "the first number is ___ times the second number."
20. £45 is compared to £15. Write £45 as a fraction of £15, simplified.
21. A tree is 2/3 the height of a lamppost. Write this comparison as a ratio, tree : lamppost.
22. Sam is 4/5 as tall as his older brother. Write this comparison as a ratio.
23. Write the ratio 9 : 12 in its simplest form, then write it as a fraction: "first amount is ___ of second amount."

## Reasoning (AO2)

24. Mia says that if A is 2/5 of B, then the ratio A : B is 2 : 5. Is she correct? Explain.

25. A model car is 1/20 the length of the real car. Priya says this means the ratio model : real is 1 : 20. Is she correct?

26. **Always, sometimes, or never?** If quantity A is written as a fraction of quantity B, that fraction must be less than 1.

27. Two numbers are in the ratio 3 : 7. Leo says the smaller number is 3/7 of the larger number. Is Leo correct? Explain using the ratio.

28. **True or false?** "A is 3/4 of B" means the same as "B is 4/3 of A." Explain your answer.

29. Archer says that a boy being "half as tall" as his dad is the same as writing the ratio boy : dad = 1 : 2. Is Archer correct?

30. Sloan has £20 and her brother has £50. Write Sloan's money as a fraction of her brother's money, and explain what the fraction tells you.

## Problem (AO3)

31. Archer's paper aeroplane flew 4 m. Sloan's flew 10 m. Write Archer's distance as a fraction of Sloan's distance, in simplest form.

32. In a school of 900 pupils, 360 are in Key Stage 2. Write the number of Key Stage 2 pupils as a fraction of the whole school, in simplest form.

33. A recipe uses 150 g sugar and 450 g flour. Write the amount of sugar as a fraction of the amount of flour, in simplest form.

34. A London bus route is 18 km long. A shorter local route is 6 km long. Write the length of the local route as a fraction of the bus route, in simplest form.

35. A child ticket costs £6 and an adult ticket costs £15. Write the cost of a child ticket as a fraction of an adult ticket, in simplest form.

36. Team A scored 24 points and Team B scored 18 points. Write Team B's score as a fraction of Team A's score, in simplest form.

37. A tank holds 40 litres when full. Right now it has 25 litres. Write the amount in the tank as a fraction of the full amount, in simplest form.

## Stretch

38. A photo is enlarged so the new width is 7/4 of the original width. If the original width was 12 cm, find the new width.

39. Two ribbons are in the ratio 5 : 8. Write the length of the shorter ribbon as a fraction of the longer, then find the shorter ribbon's length if the longer ribbon is 40 cm.

40. A number, N, is 3/8 of another number, M. If N = 27, find M.

41. In a fruit bowl, the number of apples is 2/3 of the number of oranges. There are 18 oranges. How many apples are there, and what is the ratio apples : oranges in simplest form?

42. Design your own comparison of two real-life quantities (money, distance, or weight). Express one as a fraction of the other, and also write it as a ratio in simplest form.
