## Fluency (AO1)

1. Write **7/10** as a decimal.
2. Write 0.25 as a fraction in its simplest form.
3. Write **3/5** as a decimal.
4. Shade one grid of 100 squares to show 0.35. What fraction is that in simplest form?

## Reasoning (AO2)

5. Mo says 0.8 and **4/5** are the same amount. Is Mo correct? Show your working.

6. **Always, sometimes, or never?** A decimal with one decimal place is the same as a fraction with denominator 10. Give an example if it is *sometimes* true.

## Problem (AO3)

7. Archer walks **3/4** km to school and **1/2** km to the library after school. How far does he walk in total? Give your answer as a decimal number of kilometres.

## Stretch

8. Place these on a blank 0 to 1 line: 0.2, **1/4**, 0.75, **3/10**. List them in order from smallest to largest.
