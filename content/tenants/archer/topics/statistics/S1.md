## Fluency (AO1)

1. A class was asked, “What is your favourite fruit?” The answers were: apple, banana, apple, orange, banana, apple, grape, banana, apple. Complete a tally chart for **apple**, **banana**, **orange**, and **grape**.
2. From question 1, which fruit was chosen **most often**? How many chose it?
3. Here is a bar chart title: **Favourite pet** (Cat 8, Dog 5, Fish 3, Rabbit 4). Which pet is the **least** popular? How many more chose cats than fish?
4. The table shows books read last month: **0 books — 2 pupils**, **1 book — 5 pupils**, **2 books — 4 pupils**, **3 books — 1 pupil**. How many pupils took part altogether?

## Reasoning (AO2)

5. Sam’s bar chart has a vertical axis that goes 0, 10, 20, 30 but the tallest bar only reaches **12**. Sam says the chart is wrong. Do you agree? Explain.

6. **True or false?** If every bar in a bar chart is the same height, then everyone chose a different category. Explain your answer.

## Problem (AO3)

7. On the walk to school, Archer counts types of transport in 20 minutes: **car 12**, **bus 3**, **bike 4**, **walk 1**. Draw a bar chart for this data (label axes and give your chart a title). Which type did Archer see most?

## Stretch

8. Write **three** survey questions you could ask your class. For one question, say whether answers would be **numbers** or **categories**, and how you would present the results (tally, table, or bar chart).
